Featured Project

Tuberculosis Detection from Chest X-Rays

Built and compared three CNN-based deep learning models to classify chest X-ray images as Normal or Tuberculosis. The best model (GoogLeNet) achieved 100% test accuracy on 630 images with an AUC of 1.0000. Addressed dataset imbalance (5:1 ratio) through oversampling and implemented comprehensive evaluation with ROC curves, confusion matrices, and per-class metrics.
